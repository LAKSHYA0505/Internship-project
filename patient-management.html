Patient Management

The Patient Management module is a key feature of the Hospital Management System. It enables healthcare providers to manage patient records efficiently and securely. With this system, you can:

  • Store and access patient demographics, medical history, and reports.
  • Track ongoing treatments and prescriptions.
  • Monitor patient appointments and follow-ups.
  • Ensure data privacy and security.
